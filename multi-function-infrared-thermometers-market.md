<p><p>The Multi-Function Infrared Thermometers market includes two primary types: Multi-Function Ear Infrared Thermometers and Multi-Function Forehead Infrared Thermometers. Ear infrared thermometers measure temperature via the tympanic membrane, offering quick and accurate readings, especially beneficial for infants and children. Forehead infrared thermometers, on the other hand, use an infrared sensor to gauge surface temperature without direct contact, making them suitable for a variety of age groups. Both types are increasingly popular for their convenience and non-invasive nature in health monitoring.</p></p>

<p><p>The EMI suppression polypropylene film capacitor market comprises two main types: Class X and Class Y capacitors. Class X capacitors are designed for use across the power line, providing protection against differential mode interference. They are commonly used in power supplies and electrical equipment. Class Y capacitors, on the other hand, are intended for across-the-line applications, ensuring safety by mitigating common mode noise and providing isolation from electric shock. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing device performance and compliance with electromagnetic interference standards.</p></p>

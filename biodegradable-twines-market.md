<p><p>The biodegradable twines market primarily comprises two types: PLA (Polylactic Acid) and PBAT (Polybutylene Adipate Terephthalate). PLA twines are derived from renewable resources like cornstarch and are compostable, making them suitable for agricultural and horticultural applications. In contrast, PBAT twines exhibit flexibility and durability, allowing for enhanced performance in various environments. Both types cater to the growing demand for sustainable alternatives in packaging and agricultural practices, contributing to environmentally friendly solutions in various industries.</p></p>

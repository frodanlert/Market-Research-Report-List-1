<p><strong>The Medical Pipettes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Channel Pipette</li><li>Multichannel Pipette</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical pipettes market is primarily divided into two main types: single-channel pipettes and multichannel pipettes. Single-channel pipettes are designed to transfer liquids one channel at a time, making them ideal for precise measurements in applications like research and clinical diagnostics. In contrast, multichannel pipettes can simultaneously dispense liquids through multiple channels, enhancing efficiency in high-throughput settings such as laboratories performing DNA sequencing or ELISA assays. This differentiation caters to varying workflow needs and precision requirements in medical and research environments.</p></p>

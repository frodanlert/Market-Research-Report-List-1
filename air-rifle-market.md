<p><p>The air rifle market consists of several types: Spring air rifles use a coiled spring for propulsion, offering simplicity and reliability. Gas piston air rifles utilize compressed gas, providing smoother shots and less recoil. Precharged pneumatic (PCP) air rifles are powered by high-pressure air, enabling high consistency and accuracy. CO2 powered air rifles use CO2 cartridges for propulsion, offering convenience and easy maintenance. Variable pump air rifles allow users to manually pump air for each shot, offering adjustable power and versatility.</p></p>

<p><p>The Short-wave Infrared (SWIR) camera market comprises two main types: area cameras and linear cameras. Area cameras capture wide-field images, making them ideal for applications such as surveillance, agriculture, and quality control in manufacturing. In contrast, linear cameras are designed for scanning applications, capturing images in a single line and often used for industrial inspections, material sorting, and other high-speed imaging tasks. Both types significantly enhance visibility in low-light conditions and improve material analysis across various industries.</p></p>

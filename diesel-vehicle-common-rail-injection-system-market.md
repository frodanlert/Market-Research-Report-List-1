<p><p>The Diesel Vehicle Common Rail Injection System market is segmented into low-pressure and high-pressure pump systems. Low-pressure pump systems are responsible for supplying fuel from the tank to the common rail, ensuring consistent flow and pressure for optimal performance. In contrast, high-pressure pump systems create the necessary pressure to inject fuel into the combustion chamber with precision. Both systems play crucial roles in enhancing fuel efficiency, reducing emissions, and improving engine performance in modern diesel vehicles.</p></p>

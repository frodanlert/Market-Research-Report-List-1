<p><strong>The Radiofluoroscopy System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Field</li><li>Midfield</li><li>High Field</li><li>Ultra-High Field</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Radiofluoroscopy System market is categorized into four types based on magnetic field strength. Low Field systems operate at strengths under 1.0 Tesla, suitable for general applications with lower spatial resolution. Midfield systems, ranging from 1.0 to 2.0 Tesla, offer better image quality and are ideal for a variety of diagnostic procedures. High Field systems, above 2.0 Tesla, provide superior resolution and advanced imaging techniques. Ultra-High Field systems, above 7.0 Tesla, deliver exceptional detail, primarily for research and specialized clinical applications.</p></p>

<p><p>The rubber market is primarily divided into two types: natural rubber and synthetic rubber. In contrast, synthetic rubber is manufactured from petrochemicals, offering enhanced durability and resistance to wear, which makes it suitable for automotive and industrial applications. Both markets are influenced by global supply and demand, technological innovations, and environmental considerations.</p></p>

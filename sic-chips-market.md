<p><ul><li>SiC Trench Gate Chip</li><li>SiC Planar Gate Chip</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The SiC chips market is primarily divided into two types: SiC Trench Gate Chips and SiC Planar Gate Chips. SiC Trench Gate Chips are designed with a three-dimensional structure that reduces on-resistance and enhances efficiency, making them suitable for high-power applications. In contrast, SiC Planar Gate Chips feature a simpler, two-dimensional architecture, which facilitates easier manufacturing but may have higher on-resistance. Both types cater to diverse sectors, including automotive and renewable energy, driving the growth of the SiC market.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Water-washed</li><li>Airfloat</li><li>Calcined</li><li>Delaminated</li><li>Surface-modified & Unprocessed</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The kaolin mining market comprises various types of processed kaolin. Water-washed kaolin is refined using water to remove impurities, resulting in high brightness. Airfloat kaolin is produced by air classification to achieve fine particle sizes. Calcined kaolin is subjected to high temperatures to enhance its properties. Delaminated kaolin is mechanically processed to separate platelets for improved performance. Surface-modified kaolin undergoes chemical treatments to enhance specific properties, while unprocessed kaolin refers to raw material without any treatment or refinement.</p></p>

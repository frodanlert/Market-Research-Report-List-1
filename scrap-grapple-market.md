<p><p>The scrap grapple market comprises two main types: hydraulic grapples and mechanical grapples. Hydraulic grapples utilize hydraulic systems to provide powerful and versatile gripping capabilities, making them suitable for heavy-duty tasks in recycling and demolition operations. In contrast, mechanical grapples rely on manual or mechanical mechanisms for operation, offering a simpler and often more cost-effective solution for lighter applications. Both types serve vital roles in the efficient handling of scrap materials, each catering to different operational needs and environments.</p></p>

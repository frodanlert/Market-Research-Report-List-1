<p><p>Aluminium Foam Sandwich (AFS) refers to lightweight composites consisting of a core of aluminum foam sandwiched between two outer layers of aluminum. The market is divided into two main types: Ex-situ Bonded AFS, where the foam is created separately and then bonded to the skins, offering design flexibility and easier production; and In-situ Bonded AFS, where foam is generated in place, providing stronger bonds and better durability. Both types cater to diverse industries, including aerospace, automotive, and construction.</p></p>

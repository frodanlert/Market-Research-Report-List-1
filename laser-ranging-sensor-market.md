<p><strong>The Laser Ranging Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Laser Triangulation Sensor</li><li>Time-of-Flight Laser Ranging Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Laser Ranging Sensor market is primarily divided into two types: Laser Triangulation Sensors and Time-of-Flight Laser Ranging Sensors. </p><p>Laser Triangulation Sensors utilize a laser beam to measure the distance based on the angle of reflection, providing high precision over short ranges. In contrast, Time-of-Flight Sensors calculate distance by measuring the time it takes for a emitted laser pulse to return after reflecting off an object, making them suitable for longer ranges. Both technologies are widely used in automation, robotics, and quality control applications.</p></p>

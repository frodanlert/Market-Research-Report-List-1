<p><p>Nuclear dismantling and decommissioning services encompass two primary market types: immediate and deferred dismantling. Immediate dismantling involves the rapid dismantling of nuclear facilities following their shutdown, emphasizing swift site clearance and waste management. In contrast, deferred dismantling entails a prolonged waiting period, allowing radioactive materials to decay before decommissioning begins. This approach often reduces hazards and costs associated with handling residual radioactivity, making it a strategic choice for certain facilities. Both methods aim to ensure safety, regulatory compliance, and environmental restoration.</p></p>

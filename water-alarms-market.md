<p><p>The water alarms market comprises two main types: wireless and wired water alarms. Wireless water alarms use radio frequency technology to detect leaks and send alerts without requiring extensive cabling, making them easy to install and reposition. In contrast, wired water alarms are connected through physical cables, providing a reliable power source and consistent functionality, but often requiring more complex installation. Both types serve to protect homes and businesses from water damage by providing timely alerts in case of leaks.</p></p>

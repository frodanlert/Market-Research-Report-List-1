<p><ul><li>Surface-Mounting</li><li>Throgh-hole Mounting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sealed tactile switches can be categorized into two primary market types: surface-mounting and through-hole mounting. Surface-mounting tactile switches are designed for direct placement onto the surface of printed circuit boards (PCBs), offering space efficiency and automated assembly benefits. In contrast, through-hole mounting switches involve a pin-through-hole design, providing enhanced durability and ease of manual assembly, especially in applications with high mechanical stress. Both types serve diverse electronic applications, catering to specific design and operational requirements.</p></p>

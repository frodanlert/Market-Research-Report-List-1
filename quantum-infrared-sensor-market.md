<p><p>The Quantum Infrared Sensor market can be categorized into two main types: photon detection and thermal detection. Photon detection utilizes the principles of quantum mechanics to identify individual photons, offering high sensitivity and fast response times, making it ideal for applications like spectroscopy and security systems. Conversely, thermal detection measures the infrared radiation emitted by objects based on their temperature, providing a broader detection range and being widely used in applications such as surveillance, automotive, and thermal imaging.</p></p>

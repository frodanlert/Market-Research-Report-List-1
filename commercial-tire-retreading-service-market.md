<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Commercial Tire Retreading Service Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pre-cure (Cold) Retreading</li><li>Mold Cure (Hot) Retreading</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial tire retreading service market comprises two main types: pre-cure (cold) retreading and mold cure (hot) retreading. Pre-cure retreading involves applying a pre-fabricated tread to the prepared tire casing, which is then bonded under heat and pressure. In contrast, mold cure retreading utilizes a heated mold to bond new tread to a tire casing, allowing for a more intricate tread design and enhanced performance. Both methods aim to extend tire life while offering cost-effective and eco-friendly solutions for commercial fleets.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gas flow dryer market is typically segmented into two main types: Type I and Type II. Type I involves direct gas flow drying, where hot gases come into direct contact with the material, enhancing efficient heat transfer and moisture removal. Conversely, Type II refers to indirect gas flow drying, where materials are exposed to heated surfaces or gases without direct contact, minimizing contamination risk and preserving quality. Each type has distinct applications, catering to various industries and drying requirements.</p></p>

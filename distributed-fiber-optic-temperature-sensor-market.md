<p><p>The Distributed Fiber Optic Temperature Sensor market is categorized into Multi-Mode Fiber (MMF) and Single-Mode Fiber (SMF). MMF sensors utilize a thicker core to support multiple light modes, offering advantages in short-distance applications and lower costs, making them suitable for industrial environments. In contrast, SMF sensors feature a thinner core and are designed for long-distance data transmission with minimal signal loss, ideal for critical applications such as oil and gas monitoring, structural health, and environmental sensing.</p></p>

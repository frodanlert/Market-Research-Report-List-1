<p><p>The 3D Solder Paste Inspection (SPI) System market is divided into two main types: Off-line SPI Systems and In-line SPI Systems. Off-line SPI Systems are used for inspecting solder paste application on assembled boards in a separate workstation, allowing for flexibility and thorough analysis before further processing. In contrast, In-line SPI Systems integrate directly into the production line, facilitating real-time inspection and immediate feedback, enhancing efficiency and ensuring quality control during high-speed manufacturing processes.</p></p>

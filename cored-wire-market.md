<p><p>The cored wire market is segmented into several types based on their compositions and applications. Calcium Silicide Cored Wire (CaSi) is used for deoxidation and modifying steel properties. Calcium Solid Cored Wire (Ca) serves similar functions, enhancing steel cleanliness. Pure Carbon Cored Wire (C) is utilized to adjust carbon content in steelmaking. Ferro Calcium Cored Wire (CaFe) combines calcium with iron to improve steel quality and reduce impurities. Other variations include specialized alloyed wires for niche applications.</p></p>

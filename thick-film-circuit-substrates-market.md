<p><ul><li>Single-layer Thick Film Circuit Substrates</li><li>Multilayer Thick Film Circuit Substrates</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Thick Film Circuit Substrates market is categorized into two main types: single-layer and multilayer substrates. Single-layer thick film circuit substrates consist of a single conductive layer printed onto a ceramic or glass base, providing simple circuit designs for low-density applications. In contrast, multilayer thick film circuit substrates feature multiple conductive layers, enabling complex circuit layouts and higher component density. This versatility makes multilayer substrates ideal for advanced electronics, such as medical devices and telecommunications, requiring compact design and enhanced performance.</p></p>

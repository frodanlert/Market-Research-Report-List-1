<p><strong>The Transparent EVA Films Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li> Normal</li><li>Anti-PID </li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Transparent EVA Films market is categorized into two main types: Normal and Anti-PID (Potential Induced Degradation). Normal EVA films are primarily used as encapsulants in solar panels, providing protection and durability. In contrast, Anti-PID films are designed specifically to combat potential-induced degradation, a phenomenon that can reduce the efficiency of solar cells. The Anti-PID films incorporate specialized properties that mitigate this issue, ensuring better long-term performance and reliability in solar energy systems.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Metal Detectors for Pharmaceutical Industry Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Very Low-Frequency Metal Detector (VLF)</li><li>Pulse Induction Detectors (PI)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In the pharmaceutical industry, metal detectors are crucial for ensuring product safety and quality. Very Low-Frequency (VLF) metal detectors utilize low-frequency electromagnetic fields to identify ferrous and non-ferrous metals, offering high sensitivity for small particles. Pulse Induction (PI) detectors operate by sending short pulses of current, detecting metals at greater depths, making them effective for thicker materials. Other metal detection technologies may include combination systems or advanced sensors, each tailored to meet specific industry standards and requirements for contamination control.</p></p>

In contrast, multi-layered sealing tape comprises several layers, enhancing durability, moisture resistance, and insulation properties, making it suitable for more demanding applications like industrial packaging and construction. Each type serves distinct purposes based on the required strength, flexibility, and environmental resistance in various industries.</p></p>

<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Metalcasting Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gravity Casting</li><li>High-Pressure Die Casting</li><li>Low-Pressure Die Casting</li><li>Sand Casting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The metalcasting market encompasses various techniques, each suited for specific applications. Gravity casting involves pouring molten metal into molds, relying on gravity for flow, ideal for intricate shapes. High-pressure die casting uses high pressure to inject metal into molds, ensuring precision and speed, commonly used in automotive parts. Low-pressure die casting involves forcing molten metal into molds at low pressure, suitable for large components. Sand casting utilizes sand molds, providing flexibility and cost-effectiveness for custom shapes and prototypes.</p></p>

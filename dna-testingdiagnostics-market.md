<p><strong>The DNA Testing/Diagnostics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PCR-Based Diagnostics</li><li>ISH Diagnostics</li><li>NGS DNA Diagnosis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The DNA testing/diagnostics market includes several key types. PCR-based diagnostics employ polymerase chain reaction technology to amplify DNA, enabling the detection of specific genetic material, crucial for infectious disease identification and genetic disorders. ISH (In Situ Hybridization) diagnostics involve hybridizing DNA probes to target tissues, allowing for precise localization of genetic material, useful in cancer research. NGS (Next-Generation Sequencing) provides comprehensive genetic sequencing, facilitating advanced genomic analysis for personalized medicine and complex disease understanding. Each method serves unique diagnostic purposes.</p></p>

<p><p>The ambulatory electrocardiography (ECG) market includes three main types: Resting ECG Devices, Stress ECG Devices, and Holter Monitors. Resting ECG Devices capture heart activity at rest, providing a snapshot of cardiac health. Stress ECG Devices are used during physical or pharmacological stress tests to evaluate heart function under exertion. Holter Monitors are portable devices worn for 24-48 hours or longer to continuously record heart rhythms, enabling detection of irregularities over extended periods. Together, they enhance cardiac diagnostics and monitoring.</p></p>

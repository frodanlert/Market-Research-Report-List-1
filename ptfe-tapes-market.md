<p><p>The PTFE tapes market is categorized based on density: low, standard, medium, and high. Low-density PTFE tapes are flexible and suitable for low-pressure applications. Standard-density tapes offer a balance of flexibility and strength for general sealing tasks. Medium-density tapes provide enhanced resistance and durability for more demanding environments. High-density PTFE tapes are the most robust, ideal for high-pressure and high-temperature applications, offering superior sealing properties and chemical resistance, making them essential in industrial and chemical sectors.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Phono Cartridges Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Moving Magnet (MM)</li><li>Moving Coil (MC)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The phono cartridge market primarily consists of two types: Moving Magnet (MM) and Moving Coil (MC). MM cartridges utilize a magnet attached to a cantilever that moves within a fixed coil, providing a robust output and easy compatibility with most turntables. MC cartridges, on the other hand, have a moving coil attached to the stylus, offering higher fidelity and nuanced sound quality but typically requiring specialized phono preamps. Each type caters to different audiophile preferences and budget ranges, shaping their respective market segments.</p></p>

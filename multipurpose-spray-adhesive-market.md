<p><strong>The Multipurpose Spray Adhesive Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent-Based</li><li>Water-Based</li><li>Hot Melt</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The multipurpose spray adhesive market is categorized into three main types: solvent-based, water-based, and hot melt adhesives. Solvent-based adhesives offer strong bonding and quick drying but may emit volatile organic compounds (VOCs). Water-based adhesives are eco-friendly, easy to clean, and suitable for various applications but may have slightly lower bonding strength. Hot melt adhesives provide instant adhesion through a melted state, ideal for crafts and packaging, though they require specific application equipment. Each type caters to different user needs and environmental considerations.</p></p>
